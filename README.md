## Wonder Lanka Experience - Professional Tour Operator Website

Welcome to the official repository for the **Wonder Lanka Experience** website, owned and managed by **Gayan Waruna**. This project is a highly professional, SEO-optimized, and visually stunning multi-page website designed to showcase Sri Lanka's premier destinations, custom tour plans, and private driver services.

The design utilizes a carefully curated color palette of **Beige, Light Blue, and Light Green** to evoke the tranquil beaches, lush tea plantations, and rich heritage of beautiful Sri Lanka.

---

## 🎨 Design & Aesthetic Identity

To reflect the vibrant yet serene nature of Ceylon, the user interface adheres strictly to the following color harmony:
* **Beige (`#F7F4EF`)**: Used as the primary background to represent the golden coastal sands and cultural historical monuments.
* **Light Blue (`#E6F0FA` / `#2A7FBA`)**: Represents the crystal-clear Indian Ocean waters, whale watching horizons, and open skies.
* **Light Green (`#EAF4EC` / `#3B8B56`)**: Represents the lush misty hills of Ella, tea plantations, and wildlife national parks (Yala/Udawalawe).

## 📂 Multi-Page Architecture & Content Structure

The application features **4 dedicated page tabs** with persistent smooth navigation, a responsive grid system, and interactive hover feedback animations.

### 1. 🏠 Home Page (`index.html`)
Designed to hook visitors immediately and establish trust through absolute transparency.
* **Hero Section:** High-impact heading designed for organic search engine traction. Features a background of the *Ella Nine Arch Bridge train journey*.
* **About Me (Gayan Waruna):** > *"My name is Gayan Waruna and I am the owner of Wonder Lanka Experience. I am handling this website with my team, always trying to provide the very best, unique, and transparent service as your trusted travel partner and sightseeing driver. We are travelling specialists all over Sri Lanka and we'd be happy to welcome you and show you around."*
* **How We Help You:** Clean, modern minimal graphics/logos highlighting key pillars: *Tailor-made Flexibility, 100% Transparency, Airport Pickups, and Safe Local Driving Experts.*
* **Wonder Moments (Gallery):** A high-resolution responsive grid gallery showing authentic pictures of past international guests enjoying safaris, beaches, and cultural hikes.
* **Client Portfolio & Testimonials:** Direct aggregation of five-star Google Reviews and direct client feedback verifying punctuality, friendliness, and deep local knowledge.

### 2. 🗺️ Tours Page (`tours.html`)
Showcases pre-planned comprehensive itineraries while explicitly marketing **Fully Customizable Plans** based on guest preferences.
* **3-Day Itinerary:** *Udawalawe Elephants / Ella Hills / Adam's Peak / Return to Mirissa Surfing Beaches.* (Ideal for short adventure seekings).
* **5-Day Cultural & Wildlife Special:** * *Day 1:* Airport/Negombo Pick up ➔ Drive to Sigiriya ➔ Ancient City of Polonnaruwa ➔ Evening Habarana / Kaudulla National Park Elephant Gathering Safari.
    * *Day 2-4:* Drive to Kandy via Dambulla Golden Cave Temple & Matale Aluvihare Hindu Temple ➔ Cultural Exploration.
    * *Day 5:* Down South coastal drive via Dickwella Blowhole ➔ Tour finish at beach drop-off.
* **Extended Packages:** Detailed breakdowns for **4, 8, 10, and 14-day absolute Sri Lankan journeys**.
* **Custom Plan Call-to-Action:** A standout highlight section reminding guests that *"Every itinerary can be 100% personalized to your flight times and pacing."*

### 3. 🌟 Most Popular Places Page (`popular-places.html`)
An interactive travel guide that boosts SEO through rich text descriptions and vibrant cards for iconic hotspots:
* **Sigiriya Lion Rock:** The legendary sky fortress and ancient frescoes.
* **Ella & Nine Arch Bridge:** Iconic colonial-era railway views amidst misty emerald tea hills.
* **Kandy Temple of the Tooth:** The sacred heart of Sri Lankan Buddhist heritage.
* **Yala & Udawalawe National Parks:** Majestic leopard safaris and wild elephant encounters.
* **Dambulla Cave Complex:** Stunning historic statues carved deep into rock ceilings.
* **Mirissa & Weligama:** World-class surfing breaks and blue whale watching excursions.

### 4. ✉️ Contact Us Page (`contact.html`)
An intuitive, conversion-optimized lead capture form designed to maximize direct bookings.
* **Interactive Booking Form Fields:** Name, Email, Phone Number, Selected Fixed Tour Package Dropdown (3, 4, 5, 8, 10, 14 days) **OR** Custom Plan selection.
* **Direct Contact Vectors:** Quick links for WhatsApp chat, Direct Email, and Mobile Hotline for immediate responses while tourists are on the go.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack & Layout Specifications

* **Frontend:** Semantic HTML5, CSS3 Custom Properties (Variables for fast color thematic updates).
* **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first approach using CSS Media Queries (Fluid display from small smartphone viewports up to large 4K displays).
* **Animations:** Non-intrusive CSS transformations on buttons, navigation tabs, and image cards for an ultra-premium feel.

## 🚀 How to Run the Project Locally

1. Clone or download this repository to your local directory.
2. Open `index.html` inside any modern web browser (Google Chrome, Safari, Microsoft Edge, Firefox).
3. To test the contact form functionality or deploy it live, hook the form action to any serverless mail handler like *Formspree*, *Netlify Forms*, or *Google Apps Script*.
